DIVES AND TRAINING

Here at SeaBubbles Diving we offer four different dives depending on time of day and experience.
We also offer a diver certification course if that is something that interests you .
All of our instructors are Dive Master Certified through internationally accredited PADI. This is the highest certification available.
The ratio of guests to instructor is always low at a max of 4:1. This means our service is the best! NO KA OI!
Dive tours are roughly 2 hours long total, including training and diving. We offer local transportation to and from our operation for a small fee.
Below is a brief description of our different dives and certification course.
I hope you like fresh pineapples because they're always on deck! Book now so we can gurantee you a spot on our boat.

SHALLOW DIVE (morning or afternoon)

Our shallow dive is for all divers. We dive in the morning around 10a.m. and in the afternoon around 2p.m.
Children 12 and over are allowed to dive on the shallow tours, as long as a guardian comes on the boat.
We can gurantee that you will get to see and interact with the animals. Sea Turtles are all over the place.
Dive tours are roughly 2 hours long total, including training and diving.

DEEP DIVE (morning onor afternoon)

Our deep dive is for certified divers. There are two shipwrecks that we can explore: The ______ and The _________.
Pictures are included with every dive for free. Ratios of instructor to guests are always kept low.
You are guranteed to see fish and other wildlife. Humpback whales can be seen in later months of September to November when they come closer to the shore.
We offer morning and afternoon dives 7 days a week. These dives usually take about 2 hours to complete.

TRAINING

Training to become a certified diver is also available. This program takes two days or about 8 hours to finish.
Instructors are certified Master Divers. The training includes diving in addition to written work and a test.
Certification is valid in all 50 states.
